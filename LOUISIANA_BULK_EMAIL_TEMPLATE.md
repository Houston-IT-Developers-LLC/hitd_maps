## ✉️ BULK EMAIL TEMPLATE (Copy/Paste Ready)

### Subject Line:
```
Public Records Request - Digital Parcel Boundary Data
```

### Email Body:
```
Louisiana Parish Assessors,

I am writing to request digital parcel boundary data for your parish under the
Louisiana Public Records Act (La. R.S. 44:1 et seq.).

WHAT I'M REQUESTING:
• Parcel boundary polygons (property lines/shapes only)
• Preferred formats: GeoJSON, Shapefile, File Geodatabase, or KML
• Minimum required field: Parcel ID/APN (unique identifier)
• No personal data needed (owner names, tax info, etc.)

PURPOSE:
This data will be used for a non-commercial open mapping project providing free
property boundary visualization for Louisiana residents, hunters, land developers,
and researchers. The interactive map currently covers 14 Louisiana parishes and
is publicly accessible at no cost.

DELIVERY OPTIONS (whichever is easiest for your office):
• Email attachment (if under 25 MB)
• Cloud storage link (Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive)
• Direct download from your GIS portal/website
• FTP/SFTP access to your GIS server
• Public ArcGIS REST API endpoint (if available)

FEES:
If there are any reasonable copying fees for digital data reproduction, please
notify me in advance with an invoice. Louisiana law requires minimal costs for
digital records.

TIMELINE:
Per La. R.S. 44:32, I respectfully request a response within 3 business days
acknowledging this request, with data delivery within a reasonable timeframe
(typically 1-2 weeks).

This is a legitimate public records request for a non-commercial mapping project
that will benefit Louisiana residents. If you have any questions or need
clarification, I'm happy to discuss.

Thank you for your time and assistance in making Louisiana parcel data more
accessible to the public.

Respectfully,

[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR EMAIL]
[YOUR PHONE NUMBER]

---

Note: If your parish already provides parcel data through a public GIS portal or
REST API, please share that link and I can download directly without burdening
your office.
```

## 🎁 PRO TIPS FOR MAXIMUM RESPONSES

### DO:
✅ Send Tuesday-Thursday between 9-11 AM CST (best response times)
✅ Use a professional email address (not gmail if possible)
✅ Keep tone friendly and helpful, not demanding
✅ Mention "non-commercial" and "public benefit" multiple times
✅ Offer multiple delivery options (make it easy for them)
✅ Follow up after 7 days if no response

### DON'T:
❌ Send on Monday morning or Friday afternoon
❌ Use aggressive language about "legal rights"
❌ Mention anything about commercial use
❌ Ask for owner names or confidential data
❌ Send from an obviously automated mail service
❌ Give up after first attempt
